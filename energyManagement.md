# Energy Management

## What are the activities you do that make you relax - Calm quadrant?

-   Gentle walking
-   Listening to music
-   Sitting quietly / doing nothing
-   Meditation
-   Spending time in nature
-   Deep breathing
-   Drinking tea and relaxing

## When do you find getting into the Stress quadrant?

- If i work long hours without taking breaks.
- Learning something new that feels difficult
- Working under deadlines
- Exams or preparation
- High-intensity workouts

## How do you understand if you are in the Excitement quadrant?

- I genuinely get excited about plans or events that are coming up.
-  Staying focused and enjoying work
- Trip with  friends.

## Important Takeaways from the Sleep is Your Superpower video:

   Sleep strengthens learning and memory
-   Sleep cleans toxins from the brain
-   Lack of sleep weakens immunity
-   Sleep stabilizes emotions
-   Good sleep boosts creativity
-   Poor sleep increases disease risk
-   Sleep improves performance and long‑term health

## What are some ideas that you can implement to sleep better?


- Keep your waking and sleeping hours regular each day.
- Relax by engaging in soothing activities, such as listening to music.
- Make sure the bedroom is dark, cool, and quiet.
-   Dark, cool bedroom
-   Light dinner
-   No heavy workout at night

## Brain‑Changing Benefits of Exercise

-   Exercise boosts mood chemicals
-   Improves memory and learning
-   Reduces stress and anxiety
-   Enhances focus and attention
-   Protects against brain aging
-   Improves overall brain performance

## Steps to Exercise More

-   Fix a regular exercise time
-   Start with small sessions
-   Choose enjoyable activities
-   Track progress
-   Walk more during the day
-   Exercise with friends
-   Keep clothes/equipment ready
-   Make it a routine
-   Set reminders or alarms
